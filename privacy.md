# Privacy Policy for SmartNotes

**Last Updated: September 16, 2025**

## Overview

SmartNotes ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our productivity application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally
- **Notes and Content**: All your notes, action items, and projects are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
- **AI Conversations**: Chat history and AI interactions are stored locally for context and functionality
- **Application Settings**: Your preferences, AI provider configurations, and app settings
- **Time Blocking Data**: Your productivity schedules and time management information

### Third-Party AI Provider Data
When you use AI features, your content may be sent to your selected AI provider:
- **Anthropic Claude**: For AI analysis and assistance
- **OpenAI GPT**: For creative and analytical tasks
- **Google Gemini**: For multimodal AI capabilities
- **xAI Grok**: For real-time information assistance
- **Apple Intelligence**: Processed on-device for privacy
- **Local AI**: Processed entirely offline on your device

### Calendar Integration Data
When you connect Google Calendar:
- **Calendar Events**: Read access to view and avoid scheduling conflicts
- **Event Creation**: Create calendar events from your time blocks
- **Calendar Lists**: Access your available calendars for integration selection

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Processing
- Provide core note-taking and productivity features
- Generate AI-powered insights and suggestions
- Organize and search your content
- Create time blocking schedules

### AI Provider Integration
- Send selected content to your chosen AI provider for analysis
- Receive AI-generated summaries, suggestions, and assistance
- Process natural language for action item extraction
- Provide contextual help and recommendations

### Calendar Synchronization
- Create calendar events from your time blocks
- Display upcoming events for context
- Suggest optimal scheduling times
- Avoid double-booking conflicts

## Data Security

### Local Storage Security
- All local data encrypted using Apple's standard encryption
- API keys stored securely in Apple Keychain with biometric protection
- No local data transmitted without your explicit action

### Third-Party Security
- API communications use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
- AI provider API keys protected by Touch ID/Face ID authentication
- No permanent storage of your content on our servers

### Biometric Authentication
- Touch ID/Face ID used solely for accessing stored API credentials
- Biometric data never leaves your device
- Authentication managed entirely by Apple's Secure Enclave

## Data Sharing

### We Do Not Share
- Your notes, content, or personal data with third parties for marketing
- Any information with data brokers or advertising networks
- Personal information for purposes unrelated to app functionality

### Limited Sharing
- **AI Providers**: Only content you explicitly send for AI processing
- **Google Calendar**: Only time block information you choose to sync
- **Apple**: Standard app usage analytics (if enabled in iOS settings)

## Your Data Rights

### Control Over Your Data
- **Local Data**: Complete control over all locally stored information
- **AI Provider Selection**: Choose which AI services to use or use offline options
- **Calendar Integration**: Optional and can be disconnected at any time
- **Data Export**: Export your notes and data in standard formats

### Data Deletion
- **Uninstall App**: Removes all local data from your device
- **Reset Data**: Clear all content while keeping the app installed
- **Disconnect Services**: Remove AI provider and calendar integrations
- **Selective Deletion**: Delete individual notes, conversations, or data sets

## Children's Privacy

SmartNotes is designed for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

## International Users

SmartNotes operates primarily with local storage. When using cloud AI providers, data may be processed in various countries where those providers operate. Please review each provider's privacy policy for specific location information.
